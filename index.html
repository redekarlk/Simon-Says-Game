Instructions
  1. Press the "Start Game" button to begin.
  2. Simon will produce a sequence of colors by lighting up buttons in a specific order.
  3. Watch carefully to the sequence.
  4. After Simon completes the sequence, it's your turn to repeat it.
  5. To repeat the sequence, click on the buttons in the same order that Simon lit them up.
  6. If you repeat the sequence correctly, Simon will add another color to the sequence, making it longer.
  7. If you make a mistake, the game ends.
  8. Try to beat your highest score with each new game!
